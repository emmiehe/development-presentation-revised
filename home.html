Goal

Here I will present an example of a list and aggregator interface in React - Chocolate Shop

The goal of this application is to create an online chocolate shopping page.

Design

Layout

I used a simple but clear section layout to increase usability of the webpage. There are three main sections: the navigation/control panel, the products body, and a cart on the right. Since my application's theme is holiday and chocolate, I used cookie cream color as the background of each section.

Cart Functionality

Each product has an image, a name, a price, a description, two attributes: color and size, and an action button "Add to Cart". When they click the action button, the cart on the right will update and display the total price amount and a list of lines. They can increase/decrease each line's qty or remove the line from the cart completely.

Filter Functionality

User can select filters to show only the relevant products.

Sort Functionality

User can also sort the products by their price.
